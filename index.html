About ICALT

ICALT is an annual International Conference on Advanced Learning Technologies and Technology-enhanced Learning organized by IEEE Computer Society and IEEE Technical Committee on Learning Technology. After its kick-off as IWALT in Palmerston North, New Zealand (2000), ICALT has been held in Madison, USA (2001), Kazan, Russia (2002), Athens, Greece (2003), Joensuu, Finland (2004), Kaohsiung, Taiwan (2005), Kerkade, The Netherlands (2006), Niigata, Japan (2007), Santander, Spain (2008), Riga, Latvia (2009), Sousse, Tunisia (2010), Athens, Georgia, USA (2011), Rome, Italy (2012), Beijing, China (2013), Athens, Greece (2014), Hualien, Taiwan (2015) , Austin, Texas, USA (2016),Timisoara, Romania (2017), Mumbai, India (2018).

The 19th IEEE International Conference on Advanced Learning Technologies (ICALT 2019) is scheduled on July at Maceió, Brazil.

IEEE TCLT

The IEEE Technical Committee on Learning Technology (TCLT) has been founded on the premise that emerging technology has the potential to dramatically improve learning. The purpose of this technical committee is to contribute to the field of Learning Technology and to serve the needs of professionals working in this field. The IEEE Technical Committee on Learning Technology (TCLT) has initiated a number of activities to promote research and development of Advanced Learning Technologies. These activities foster collaboration among academic and professional communities. Click hereto learn about IEEE TCLT in greater detail.

Call for papers

We invite submission of papers reporting original academic or industrial research in the area of Advanced Learning Technologies and Technology-enhanced Learning. All papers will be double-blindly peer-reviewed.

The anticipated types of submissions for all tracks are:

  • Full paper: 5 pages
  • Short paper: 3 pages
  • Poster paper: 2 pages

Regarding the Doctoral Consortium, the anticipated type of submission is:

  • Doctoral Consortium paper: 3 pages

Important Note: These page limits include authors' information, abstract (no more than 200 words), all tables, figures, references, etc.

IEEE TCLT Student Grant
IEEE TCLT and ICALT 2018 announce grants for student authors to participate in the vibrant community of emerging ICALT researchers.


Guidelines
  1. Student authors of papers accepted in ICALT 2018 are eligible to apply. Preference will be given to first authors of full papers and DC authors. Preference will be given to graduate students (doctoral or masters).
  2. Students will be awarded a reimbursement of up to US $500 for travel, accommodation and registration expenses.
  3. Students who have been awarded the grant must attend the conference in person and present receipts of the expenses to claim the reimbursement.

How to Apply
  • A proof of student affiliation to an academic institution (eg. photo/scan of valid I-cards or a letter from advisor) will have to be uploaded while filling the form.
  • Applicants are requested to fill this form to apply for the grant.
Submission deadline
09th April, 2019
Notification

Results will be notified to the selected applicants via the e-mail address provided during the application submission process by 20ht April, 2019.
