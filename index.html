Maceió



Voted the most beautiful city in Brazil, Maceió has lush beaches and natural pools that attract tourists worldwide. Its tropical climate and its large coconut trees give a magical touch to a paradisiacal landscape. Its cuisine is a special attribute, with regional dishes such as stuffed "tapioca", dry meat "escondidinho", and seafood. A remarkable culture that brings together art, music, crafts, dance and merriment that make up the picture of the state of Alagoas. An ocean coast that offers bars and restaurants with varied attractions and a hotel networks ready to provide comfort and warmth to its visitors.
